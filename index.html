The problem

Your top-line number is lying to you.

Three things stand between what platforms report and what you actually earn.

Fees and refunds eat your revenue

Processing fees, chargebacks, and refunds are deducted after the sale — so the number you celebrated isn't the number that lands.

Income scattered across platforms

Payouts arrive from a dozen places on a dozen schedules. Stitching them together in a spreadsheet is slow, manual, and easy to get wrong.

FX changes your numbers after the fact

Cross-currency payouts settle at rates you didn't set. By the time funds convert, your reported total and your bank balance no longer match.
